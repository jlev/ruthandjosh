I live in San Francisco or the East Bay. Should I get a hotel in Marin?

A cab between Tiburon and the Mission runs about $55 ($75 to Downtown Oakland). This will be less than any hotel nearby.

Who can I split a cab with?

Fill out this form to find potential ride shares within our guest list.

What is the dress code?

Official answer: Californian outdoor-friendly semi-formal. The ceremony and festivities all will be outside, so ladies should consider the prospect of walking on soft ground when selecting shoes (dancing will be on a brick patio). There are short trails and a beach on the site, so be prepared and wear something a little more comfortable if you'd like to go exploring. We'll keep an eye on the forecast, but expect a California sunshine and cool air at night.

Children and plus ones?

Children are welcome to the ceremony and reception. There's plenty to see and explore, so they should have a good time. Bring your serious-ish significant other if you've got one.

I am allergic to ___. Will you have anything for me to eat?

Yes. We are already aware of some gluten allergies, and will have plenty of gluten-free and vegetarian options. Note your needs on the RSVP form, and we'll make sure you're covered.

Your drawings and website are so cute; who made them?
