Weather

The coldest winter I ever spent was a summer in San Francisco. Commonly attributed to Mark Twain, this is a Bay Area resident's favorite and least helpful piece of advice for an unprepared and miserably cold toursit. "Summer in California" sounds warm, and the sky may be blue, but it will be cold. Check the forecast and bring a jacket.

Hotel Option 1: Mill Valley Inn

The Mill Valley Inn is in the center of walkable downtown Mill Valley, with several shops and restaurants, and adjacent to a brewery, which will host the post-reception after party.

Hotel Option 2: The Lodge at Tiburon

The Lodge is 1200 feet from the Tiburon Ferry Terminal, and three miles from the wedding venue along a coastal walking and bicycle path.
